Our Story

Company Name & Legal Structure

Antique Auction System Ltd. is a registered limited company, established under the laws of the United Kingdom.

Mission Statement

"To connect discerning collectors with the world's most exquisite and authenticated antique treasures, fostering a secure, transparent, and vibrant marketplace for history's finest pieces."

History & Background

Founded in 1995 by a group of passionate antique dealers and tech innovators, Antique Auction System began as a small online forum for enthusiasts to trade rare collectibles. Recognizing the growing demand for a trustworthy and accessible platform for high-value antiques, we officially launched our full-fledged auction system in 1998.

Over the decades, we've grown to become a leading name in online antique auctions, facilitating thousands of successful transactions and helping countless collectors find their dream pieces. Key milestones include introducing our proprietary authentication process in 2005, launching our mobile app in 2012, and expanding our global reach to over 100 countries by 2020.

Our Platform

Types of Auctions

Timed Auctions (Ascending Bid)

Our most common format, where items are open for bidding for a set period (e.g., 7 days). Bidders place increasingly higher bids until the timer expires, with the highest bid winning.

Live Auctions

Experience the excitement of real-time bidding with a live auctioneer. These events are scheduled, and bids are placed in quick succession, often with video streaming.

Sealed Bid Auctions (Coming Soon)

Bidders submit their best and final offer in secret. The highest bid wins, but bidders do not know what others have offered until the auction closes.

Buy Now (Fixed Price)

For select items, users have the option to purchase immediately at a fixed price, bypassing the bidding process.

Security Measures

Your security is our top priority. We employ industry-leading measures to protect your data and transactions.

  • SSL Encryption: All data transmitted on our platform is secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.
  • Secure Payment Gateways: We partner with PCI DSS compliant payment processors to handle all financial transactions.
  • KYC Verification: For high-value transactions, we implement Know Your Customer (KYC) protocols to prevent fraud.
  • Fraud Detection Systems: Advanced algorithms continuously monitor for suspicious activity.
  • Escrow Services: Optional escrow for high-value items provides an independent third-party to hold funds until both buyer and seller are satisfied.

Our Commitment to You

Transparency & Fairness

We are committed to maintaining a transparent and fair auction environment for all participants.

  • Unbiased Operations: Our platform operates with automated systems to ensure impartiality in bidding and auction closing.
  • Bidding Integrity: Strict policies are in place to prevent shill bidding, bid manipulation, and other unfair practices.
  • Clear Rules: All auction rules, fees, and terms are clearly communicated upfront.

User Protection

We strive to create a safe community where users can bid and sell with confidence.

  • Anti-Scam Measures: Robust systems and manual reviews are in place to detect and prevent fraudulent listings or user behavior.
  • Dispute Resolution: In the rare event of a dispute, our dedicated team provides mediation and resolution services to ensure a fair outcome for both parties.
  • Buyer & Seller Ratings: A transparent rating system helps users assess the reliability of other participants.
